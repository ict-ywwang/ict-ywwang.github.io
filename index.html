Biography

         Dr. Yuwei Wang is a faculty member at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, China, and also holds a position at Big Data Academy, Zhongke, China. He is the vice chair of China Communications Standards Association (CCSA) TC1 WG1, the associate rapporteur at the International Telecommunication Union Telecommunication (ITU-T) SG16 Q5, and the director of Smart Connected Big Data Research Center as well as Standards Management Office of Big Data Academy, Zhongke, China.

         Dr. Yuwei Wang received his Ph.D. degree from Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2019. He led the setting of the first ITU international standard and the first key communication standard for Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He was recognized with the "100 Stars Program" of Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and also won the silver prize (Rank 3/911) in the ITU AI/ML in 5G Global Technology Challenge, and accumulated 2 awards for innovation standard intellectual property achievements from the Chinese Academy of Sciences. He has led and participated in 20+ national or corporate sponsored research projects, including China's Next Generation Internet Projects, National Science and Technology Major Projects, and Key Projects of National Natural Science Foundation of China, etc. He led the setting of 18 ITU international standards, of which 13 have been published. He also led and participated in the setting of 20+ national professional standards related to communication industry, of which 16 have been promulgated and implemented. He has authorized 20+ national invention patents, 7 of which are core technology invention patents. He also published 20+ technical papers related to mobile computing and edge intelligence, including top journals and conferences such as TPDS, TMC, INFOCOM, etc. He is the an specially-invited expert of Z-park Strategic Alliance of Smart City Industry Technology Inn Ovation, and an expert of national, industry, local and group standard evaluation of Ministry of Industry and Information Technology.
